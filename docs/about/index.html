Aragon ZK Research started in January 2022 as the research arm of Aragon Association, a prominent web3 project focused on developing decentralised autonomous organization (DAO) software, enabling the creation and management of decentralised entities on blockchain technology. In February 2023, we became an autonomous entity named "AZKR Research Association", funded by a grant from Aragon Association with the purpose of helping develop the Aragon Project. Following the decision to dissolve Aragon Association, AZKR has become an entirely independent entity

Our website is https://azkr.org/
